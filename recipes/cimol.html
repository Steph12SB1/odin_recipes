Cimol

Simple and delicious crispy Indonesian snack!

Ingredients:

  • 500g of tapioca Kanji flour
  • 3 tsb of flour
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsb of Masako powder
  • 1/2 tsb pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Chilli powder to taste
  • 200 ml of water

Steps:

  1. Crush the garlic with the salt
  2. Boil water and put in the crushed garlic
  3. Mix the tapioca flour, pepper, and Masako powder together while slowly pouring in the boiled water
  4. Mix or knead the dough until it has a smooth texture
  5. Form the dough into marble sized balls
  6. Fry the dough in the oil before the oil heats up
  7. Fry the balls of dough till it starts to expand, and stir it around to make sure it cooks evenly
  8. You'll know they're done when they have a nice white color and have expanded quite a bit in size
  9. Serve your cimol with the chilli powder or any flavoring you want and enjoy
